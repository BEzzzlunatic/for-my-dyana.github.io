DANCE OF THE HEARTSTRINGS

Your face, your form, your being,
All lure me to you like a melody.
Your voice, a siren’s calling,
All I’ll do is give in to your harmony.
Your fingers play with my heart strings,
Each strum makes me dance to your piece,
An alluring piece that in my ears it lingers,
Each strum pulls me in without a cease.

PRISONER OF THE ROSE

A wandering wind carried a rose to my deserted heart,
Petals serenely unfurling to allure me towards her,
A fragrance of its own kind holds me captive forever,
Because I am a prisoner of that fragrance, I crave its heart,
And I dare to grasp the thorns because I want her only mine.

STRUMS OF LOVE

When unbearable burdens wrap me with a shroud,
Melodies from you cradle me with gentle warmth,
Unwrapping my soul to weave it back again aloud,
With her strings filling my void, keeping me going forth.

Despair fills my heart but each strum veils it in shadow,
Bathing my heart in love with each note that it flows.

I LOVE PARIS

I love Paris because my love is there,
A love I never thought I’d find, so rare,
Has won through my walls like Samothrace,
And that with her melodies gives me a warm embrace,
Her voice luring me in like a sailor,
And I fall into her seas as I’m chained to her,
Forever.

HOW FAR?

How far would I go to see you smile?
I'd trade the sun, the sky, the air,
Surrender breath for just a glance,
And still, I’d call it fair.

If time stood cold and kept us apart,
I’d wager life itself for thee,
Let Heaven cast me to the dark,
If love could set you free.

LOVE AGAIN

And when then I gave up on love,
Because nothing was left in me,
And not even the gods above -
Could dare to change my fate,
Built my walls and locked the door,
No longer seeking nor wanting more,

But then you came in strong,
And made me a song.

Now love’s a fire I can’t contain,
A relentless, endless rain,
In which I drown.

SWEETEST PLEASURE

Because when I see your face,
I have the sweetest pleasure,
A keeper of the greatest treasure,

Her cheeks, softer than a rose’s kiss,
In her eyes — the universe lies,
Endless vast and sealed with bliss,
Her form, like Samothrace’s,
A timeless grace, a work of art,
Her voice, a melody I ache to hear.
It draws me in, holds me near and dear.

A SPRING’S FRESH BREEZE

Comes to me like spring’s fresh breeze,
Her scent dances in the breeze with ease,
And with a graceful dance enfolds me,
Now from her I can never flee.
